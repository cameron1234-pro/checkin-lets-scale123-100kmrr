CheckIn helps you stay connected and safer during commutes, meetups, travel, and daily routines.

Create quick check-in protocols, assign trusted emergency contacts, and update your status in real time.

Key features:
- Start and manage active check-ins in seconds
- Set trusted emergency contacts
- One-tap SOS button for urgent situations
- Danger alert status that notifies emergency contacts
- Check-in history for past protocols
- Simple onboarding and settings

Whether you're heading home late, meeting someone new, or checking in during travel, CheckIn gives you a fast way to share status and trigger help when needed.
